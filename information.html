Unbox FAQ

General Information

What is Unbox?

  • This is UltraBox but with my ideas added on. This also is sort of a testing ground for me to learn code.
  • Some ideas come from how sound chips work and that and many ideas may seem excessive but pushing limits is fun.

Who is this mod for?

  • This mod is for anyone regardless of skill level, I strongly suggest checking out UltraBox first though.

Why does Unbox exist?

  • It exists because I wanted it to exist. I got tired of asking "what if X existed" or "why can't W, X, Y apply to Z as well".

Why is the b in Unbox not a capitalized B? Why is it not called UnBox or UNBox?

  • Because I said so and I found it weird having it spelled that way.

What does Unbox add, why it exists and how does it work?

+-24 Pitch Shift range

  • I felt the range was too small so I increased it. It still works the same as normal Pitch Shift but with more range.

Pulse Width range

  • The fact that 1%-50% just felt limiting. With what I added, 0-100% is possible if you include the decimal offset. There is just so much that is possible with that range increase.
  • Lets ignore the fact that pulse width is Inverted and that the original range was really 50%-99%. That problem also applies to Custom chip.

Duty Cycle

  • I wanted to revive a function from an old mod called SandBox and that mod had Duty Cycle but it was limited to a few pulse widths and only 2 cycle types.
  • What I did differently was increased the amount of pulse width values and allows the user to select the cycle length and speed. (insert UI stuff when finished)

Shift F shortcut

  • How many times did you want to jump to the start of the loop? All the time? Yeah, that is why I made this shortcut. It even was put in UltraBox!

Tempo range increase

  • Ever wanted to make songs that are faster than 500 "tempo"? That would limit you to about 200Hz. I increased it to 1000 so 400Hz overclock should be available. I would like to increase it to 10k at one point for 1kHz.
  • You still can edit the tempo via the text box.
  • I intend to create a clock-speed type of option that may make the Hz more accurate. With 60Hz being the standard.
  • I also added new rhythms that should help out if you increase the beats per bar in tandem with higher tempo.

Chipwaves

  • Unbox adds some chipwaves from some sound chips like the AY-3-8910 and the SID-6581/8580. Those can always be improved, let me know if it needs updating.

Presets

  • Unbox has new presets. Ranging from ones that come from chips or ones with sentimental value.
