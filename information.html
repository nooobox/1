Noobox FAQ

General Information

What is Noobox?

  • This is UltraBox but with my ideas added on. This also is sort of a testing ground for me to learn code.
  • Some ideas come from how sound chips work and that and many ideas may seem excessive but pushing limits is fun.

Who is this mod for?

  • This mod is for anyone regardless of skill level, I strongly suggest checking out UltraBox first though.

Where can you contact me?

  • The BeepBox Discord, you can probably find me in a event or in the channels related to BeepBox mods.

When will updates come out?

  • Updates will happen whenever things are ready, I WILL delay updates if you keep asking about it. (-:< (Jk)
  • Updates generally release when features are done or when bug fixes are applied. There might be micro updates but don't mind those.

Why does Noobox exist?

  • It exists because I wanted it to exist. I got tired of asking "what if X existed" or "why can't W, X, Y apply to Z as well".
